The Artist Behind Breathing Brushes

Hello! I'm Ankita Tiwari, the founder and artist behind Breathing Brushes. What began as a childhood passion for creating beautiful things has blossomed into a full-fledged art studio based in Dubai.

My journey started when I was just 10 years old, fascinated by how simple materials could transform into something extraordinary. Over the years, I've developed a unique style that blends traditional techniques with contemporary designs.

At Breathing Brushes, we believe art should be accessible to everyone. That's why we create pieces that resonate with both seasoned collectors and first-time art buyers.
